ABOUT US

Our team of dedicated electrical engineering students of AISSMS Institue of Information Technology, Pune has come together to create a platform 'Electrosync' that puts the power of real-time energy monitoring in your hands. As a diverse group of individuals, we seamlessly blended our skills in web development, software engineering, user experience design, and database management to bring you a website that provides live readings of electric smart meters. Our user-friendly interface, crafted with a keen eye for design and functionality, allows you to effortlessly access and understand the live data from your smart meters. From the intuitive design of the front-end to the meticulous coding of the back-end, every aspect of our website reflects our dedication to delivering a seamless experience for you.
